SwifterX connects customers with skilled, vetted home-service professionals — on demand.

Book a cleaner, plumber, electrician, or handyman in minutes. Track your provider in real time, pay securely through the app, and leave a review when the job is done.

CUSTOMERS
• Browse hundreds of local, background-checked providers
• Book same-day or schedule in advance
• Real-time GPS tracking while your provider is en route
• Pay with Apple Pay or any major card — powered by Stripe
• Full order history and one-tap rebooking
• In-app chat with your provider

PROVIDERS
• Set your own schedule and service radius
• Accept or decline jobs from your job inbox
• Mark jobs in-progress and complete them from the app
• Receive payouts directly to your bank account via Stripe
• Build your reputation with verified customer reviews

EMPOWERMENT THROUGH OPPORTUNITY
SwifterX is built for both sides of the marketplace — giving customers convenience and providers a reliable, flexible income stream.
