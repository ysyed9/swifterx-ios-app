Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service?

Open SwifterX, tap a service category on the Home tab, choose a provider, select your date and time, add any special instructions, and proceed to checkout. Payment is processed securely through Stripe.

Can I cancel or reschedule a booking?

Yes. Go to the Orders tab, find your booking, and tap Cancel. Cancellations made more than 2 hours before the scheduled time are fully refunded. For rescheduling, cancel and rebook at your preferred time.

When will I be charged?

Your card is charged when you confirm the booking. If the provider cancels or you cancel in the eligible window, you'll receive a full refund within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank.

My payment failed — what should I do?

Check that your card details are correct and that your card isn't expired. You can add a new payment method in Account → Wallet. If the problem persists, contact your bank or email support@swifterx.app.

How do I track my provider?

Once your provider marks the job as "In Progress", a live map appears at the top of your order detail. You'll also receive a push notification when they're on their way.

How do I leave a review?

After your order is marked Completed, a review prompt will appear on the order detail screen. Tap the star rating, write an optional comment, and submit. Reviews cannot be edited after submission.

I want to become a provider. How do I sign up?

Download SwifterX and create an account. On the Role Select screen, choose Provider. Follow the onboarding steps: choose your services, set your hourly rate and radius, add your availability, and complete background check consent. Once approved, you'll start receiving job requests.

How do providers get paid?

Payouts are processed via Stripe Connect. Set up your bank account in Account → Payouts & Earnings. After each completed job, your net earnings (minus the SwifterX platform fee) are automatically transferred to your bank account — typically within 2 business days.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Account → Settings → Delete Account in the app. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Alternatively, email privacy@swifterx.app and we will process your request within 30 days.

How do I report a problem with a job?

Please email support@swifterx.app with your order ID (found in the Orders tab) and a description of the issue. We aim to respond within 24 hours and will work to resolve disputes fairly for both customers and providers.

Is my personal data safe?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell your personal data. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
