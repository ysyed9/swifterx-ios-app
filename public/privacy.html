Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2026  ·  Last Updated: April 1, 2026

SwifterX ("we", "us", or "our") operates the SwifterX mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use and protect it, and your rights over your data.

By using the App you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account registration: name, email address, password (hashed), optional profile photo.
  • Provider onboarding: service categories, hourly rate, service radius, bio, availability schedule, background-check consent.
  • Booking: service address, special instructions, scheduled date and time.
  • Payments: payment method selection (processed by Stripe — we never store raw card numbers).
  • Reviews & chat: text content of reviews and in-app messages.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Precise location: collected while a job is in progress (provider shares live location; customer can view it).
  • Coarse location: used to show nearby providers and sort search results.
  • Device identifiers: push notification token (FCM) for order-status alerts.
  • Usage data: screen views and events via Firebase Analytics (anonymised).
  • Crash data: stack traces and device info via Firebase Crashlytics.

3. Data Sharing

We never sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Stripe — payment processing. Stripe's privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy.
  • Google Firebase — database, authentication, analytics, crash reporting. firebase.google.com/support/privacy.
  • Other users — providers see your first name and service address for confirmed bookings. Customers see provider name, photo, rating, and live location during a job.
  • Law enforcement — if required by a valid legal request.

4. Data Retention

  • Account data: retained until you delete your account.
  • Order history: retained for 7 years for financial compliance.
  • Analytics & crash data: 14 months (Firebase default).
  • Location data: provider live location is not stored permanently — it is used only during the job and discarded when the job completes.

5. Your Rights

Depending on where you live you may have the right to:

  • Access a copy of your personal data.
  • Correct inaccurate data (via Account → Personal Info in the app).
  • Delete your account and associated data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Data portability (receive your data in machine-readable form).

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@swifterx.app. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Children

SwifterX is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us immediately and we will delete it.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data: TLS in transit, AES-256-equivalent encryption at rest (Firebase), bcrypt password hashing, and role-based Firestore security rules. No method is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. If we make material changes, we will notify you via the App or email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
